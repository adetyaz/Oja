About Oja

What We Do

Oja serves as a local platform that connects consumers with their favourite stores. We aim to make the shopping experience easier and more pleasant with an easy to use mobile platform. With Oja all you need is a few taps

Our story

OJA is an E-commerce company, offering a third-party online market platform for supermarkets to provide their products and services to consumers. We are a grocery delivery platform facilitating doorstep deliveries of groceries and other home essentials in Nigeria. OJA profers a solution to the widespread issue of poor accessibility to groceries and resources, primarily in Nigeria. As a solution to this problem, OJA connects customers to supermarkets in close proximity, via a third party app that provides an efficient ordering service at minimal cost to both parties involved; Customers/ Supermarkets. Oja is a digital marketplace for daily shopping, Customers can shop for Grocery, Fruits, Toiletries, Baby care and other products with just few taps. They can get everything delivered instantly or schedule it for a convenient time later.
